ABOUT TOMORROW X TOGETHER

Tomorrow X Together, also known as TXT, is a South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment (now known as HYBE Corporation). The group made their highly anticipated debut in 2019 and quickly gained recognition for their vibrant music, youthful energy, and captivating performances. TXT has been making waves in the K-pop industry with their unique blend of pop, hip-hop, and R&B.

TXT's name represents their mission to provide comfort and connection to their listeners through their music. Each member showcases distinct talents and personalities, contributing to the group's cohesive and dynamic performances. With their fresh and relatable approach, TXT has garnered a dedicated fanbase both in South Korea and internationally.
